Our Story

Founded in 2020, OneCall emerged from a simple observation: local businesses needed better tools to compete in the digital age. Our founders, former small business owners themselves, experienced firsthand the challenges of growing a local business. They assembled a team of technology experts and business strategists to create a comprehensive solution that would level the playing field for local entrepreneurs.

Our mission is to empower local businesses with enterprise-level tools and strategies that are both powerful and easy to use. We believe in the importance of strong local economies and are committed to helping small businesses thrive. Our core values of innovation, accessibility, and customer success drive everything we do, from product development to customer support.
